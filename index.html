About Me

I am Lui Chun Nam Brian, an emerging artist and filmmaker based in Hong Kong, currently pursuing my BA in Creative Media at City University of Hong Kong (CityU). My versatile work spans across installation art, moving images, scriptwriting, and photography. I'm driven by themes of silence, human connection, and the unsettling aspects of urban life. With a keen eye for cinematography and narrative, I strive to craft immersive experiences that invite contemplation and emotional engagement. This website serves as a visual index of my creative journey.

Type: Interactive Art Game (Individual Work)

An interactive experience inspired by the dichotomy of choice, reminiscent of the red and blue pills from 'The Matrix'. This piece questions the nature of memory, regret, and new beginnings through a minimalist, retro-game interface.

Silence Is The Noisiest (2023)

Type: Installation Art & Moving Image (Group Work)

Role: Director, Screenwriter, Videographer, Editor, Colorist

Idea: We often pay attention to different sounds, but moments of complete silence can be the most impactful. When there is no sound, the brain may create imaginary sounds, leading to confusion. The installation uses aluminum wire to create a textured indoor environment, inviting the audience to share a contemplative ambiance.

The Other Side (2023)

Type: Short Film (Group Work)

Role: Assistant Director, Cinematographer

Synopsis: A story of peculiar feelings and emotions that develop between a student and a tutor. Faced with a clash between moral principles and their developing emotions, the teacher finds herself in a dilemma and must make a choice.

Ngau Tau Kok Subway Murder (2022)

Type: Short Film (Group Work)

Role: Director, Script Writer, Cinematographer, Leading Actor, Editor

Synopsis: Set against the backdrop of Hong Kong's enigmatic disappearances, this film intertwines dark humor with the city's unsettling past. An assassin's macabre secret is discovered by a curious security guard, leading to a grimly ironic conclusion.

Mr. Lam (2023)

Type: Script Writing (Individual Work)

Synopsis: Mr. Lam, after facing setbacks, pursues his dream of becoming a teacher at a women's college. He overcomes social anxiety to connect with his students, but a misunderstanding leads to his unjust dismissal. Years later, he is reunited with a former student, now a colleague, to pursue their shared dedication to education.
